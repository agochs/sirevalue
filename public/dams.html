How it works. We aggregate every public sale appearance by dam name, normalized for casing and country suffixes (so "Heavenly Love" and "Heavenly Love (USA)" fold together). Dams whose names normalize to fewer than 4 characters are excluded as scrape-truncation artifacts. Progeny are bucketed by foal year (yearling sale year minus 1, 2YO sale year minus 2). Matched pinhook pairs come from the realized pinhook dataset. Source: Keeneland, Fasig-Tipton, OBS.
