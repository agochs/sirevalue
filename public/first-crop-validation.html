First-Crop Validation · receipts for every forecast

For every first-crop sire we forecast a pinhook lift on, this page compares the prediction to whatever realized data has accumulated as their first crops have moved from yearling to 2YO sales. Proven rows have at least 3 realized pinhook pairs; Pending rows are forecasts still waiting for their first 2YOs to hit the ring.

Predictions are made ~12 months before realized data is possible (foals must be born, sold as yearlings, then sold again as 2YOs). The longer this page stays up, the more rows graduate from Pending to Proven — and the more the platform's forecast track record stops being a claim and starts being a record.
