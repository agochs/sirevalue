Value Gap · price-to-score outliers

How this works. For every stallion with a published fee, we compute the median Value Score of all stallions in the same fee band. Value Gap = stallion's score − band median. Positive = scoring better than peers at the same price (likely undervalued). Negative = scoring worse (likely overvalued).

This is the lens for the question "is this fee fair?". The further a stallion sits from their band median, the louder the signal in either direction.
